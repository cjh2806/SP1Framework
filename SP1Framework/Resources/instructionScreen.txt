========================== Maze Game ==========================
- Your Character is blinking in 'Green'                        
- Use 'Up', 'Down', 'Left' and 'Right' Keys to move            
- Try to get to the 'Pink' coloured end point within 30 seconds
- Take more than 30 seconds and you'll lose a life             
- Score 50 points for completing the game                      
                                                               
================= Random Number Guessing Game =================
- Guess the Number by typing into the input                    
- Press the 'Enter' key to confirm your input                  
- Score 100 points for completing the game in less than 5 tries
- Score 50 points for completing the game in more than 5 tries 
- More than 10 tries and you'll lose a life
